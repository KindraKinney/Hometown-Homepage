Visit Knoxville

A nature-loving adventure-seeking artsy kinda town.

Top three activities to do in Knoxville

Visit the Sunsphere at the World's Fair Park

Take the elevator in the Sunsphere to enjoy spectacular views of Knoxville, Tennessee River, University of Tennessee, and the Smoky Mountains.

Walk the trails at Ijams Nature Center

The Ijams Nature Center is the perfect place for a hike, bike, kayaking or swimming.

Tour the Blount Mansion

Tour the home of William Blount, one of the founders of Tennessee and one of the state's first senators.
